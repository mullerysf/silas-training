⛳ Golf Practice Guide

Short game first — that is where scores are made! Full swing comes after.

🏠 At Home
⛳ At the Range/Course
🏠 Home Practice ~20–25 min
🎯 Putting — 10 min
🏋️‍♀️
Gate Drill
Set 2 tees just wider than your putter face. Roll 20 putts through without hitting them.
💡 Straight stroke = more putts made
📏
Distance Ladder
Targets at 3 ft, 6 ft, 10 ft. Hit 5 putts at each. Count your makes and try to beat it!
🔄
Circle Drill
6 balls in a circle 3 feet from a target. Make all 6 before moving on.
💡 Builds pressure putting — short putts win holes!
🎪 Swing Mechanics — 10 min (foam balls)
🪷
Slow-Motion Swings
10 swings at 25% speed in a mirror. Club stays low on takeaway, do not pick it up steep.
⚖️
Weight Shift Drill
Back foot on backswing, front foot on follow-through. Repeat 15x with a short iron.
💡 Weight shift = distance without swinging harder
🏋️‍♀️
Foam Ball Chip Shots
Chip 20 balls at a target. Hands ahead of ball, weight on front foot.
💪 Golf Fitness — 5 min
🔄
Trunk Rotations with Club
Club across shoulders. Rotate slowly left and right x20. Feel the stretch.
💡 More rotation = more power
🧘
Hip Hinge Drill
Club against spine. Bend forward from hips (not waist) x15. This is your golf posture.
⛳ Range/Course Session ~45–60 min
🎯 Putting Green — 15 min
📏
Lag Putting
20 ft, 30 ft, 40 ft — 5 putts each. Goal: stop within 2 feet every time.
💡 Lag putting prevents 3-putts
🎯
5-Hole Make Challenge
Pick a hole ~5 feet away. Make 3 in a row before moving to the next one.
🏋️ Chipping Green — 15 min
🎯
Up-and-Down Game
Chip from ~10 yards, then putt. Count how many you get in 2 shots out of 10.
💡 Getting up and down saves strokes!
🤲
Bump-and-Run vs Lob
Same spot: 7-iron low roller vs wedge high chip. 5 each. See which works better.
🏋️‍♀️ Driving Range — 20 min
🎯
Warm-Up Wedges
10 easy half-swings with a wedge. No rushing — just feel the contact.
📊
Structured Practice
10 balls each: 7-iron, 5-iron, hybrid, driver. Pick a target for every shot.
💡 Always aim at something specific
🔄
Tempo Drill
Count 1-2-3 backswing, 4 at top, 5-6 downswing. Hit 10 balls this way.
💡 Smooth tempo = straighter shots every time
🏆 Golf Goals This Summer
⛳ Make putts consistently inside 5 feet
🎯 Get up and down from around the green
💪 Build a repeatable, smooth full swing
📏 Learn your distances with each club
😄 Stay calm, have fun, and enjoy the game!

🌭 Fuel Your Body Right!

What you eat is just as important as how you train. Food is your fuel — the better you eat, the better you feel, move, and perform on the court and course!

☀️ Why Breakfast Changes Everything
When you sleep, your body uses up its energy stores. By morning, your tank is low — like trying to drive a car with no gas. Eating breakfast refills your fuel tank so your brain can focus, your muscles can move, and you can play your best tennis and golf!
🧠
Sharper Focus
Your brain runs on glucose from food. Skip breakfast and your concentration drops — bad for school AND for watching a tennis ball!
⚡
More Energy All Day
A good breakfast keeps your energy steady instead of crashing mid-morning. No more feeling tired and grumpy before lunch!
💪
Stronger Muscles
Protein at breakfast helps your muscles recover from yesterday and get ready for today. This is how you actually get stronger!
🏃️‍♀️
Better Athletic Performance
Athletes who eat breakfast are faster, more agile, and make fewer mistakes. Your body simply performs better when it is fueled up.
🏞 Great Breakfast Ideas
Mix and match — aim for protein + carbs + fruit every morning!
🥛
Greek Yogurt Parfait
Greek yogurt + granola + berries + a drizzle of honey
💡 Packed with protein to build muscle. The berries give you antioxidants that help recovery!
🥚
Eggs Any Style
Scrambled, fried, or in an omelette with veggies + toast
💡 Eggs are one of the best muscle-building foods there is. Add spinach for extra iron!
🍞
Peanut Butter Toast
Whole grain toast + peanut or almond butter + banana slices
💡 Great combo of protein, healthy fat, and natural sugar for quick energy
🍲
Oatmeal Power Bowl
Oatmeal + milk + banana + a spoonful of peanut butter
💡 Slow-releasing energy that keeps you fueled for hours. Great before a workout!
🍌
Smoothie + Toast
Blend banana, berries, milk, and a scoop of yogurt. Drink with a slice of toast.
💡 Perfect if you are not very hungry in the morning — easy to drink and still nutritious!
🍓
Cottage Cheese + Fruit
Cottage cheese + pineapple or peaches + a handful of granola
💡 High protein, low sugar, and the fruit gives natural energy. Super quick to make!

🚫 Foods to Limit
❌ Sugary cereals — they are mostly sugar with almost no protein or fiber. Your body digests them super fast, so you feel hungry again within an hour and your energy crashes right when you need it most. A bowl of sugary cereal is basically candy for breakfast!
❌ Skipping breakfast — always worse than eating something!
❌ Soda and juice — full of sugar with no real nutrition
❌ Chips and candy as snacks — empty calories with no fuel value
✅ Water is always the best drink — before, during, and after activity!
🎯 Snack Smart
If you are hungry between meals, reach for:

🍎 Apple slices + peanut butter
🧌 A handful of trail mix or nuts
🥛 String cheese or yogurt
🥕 Carrots or celery with hummus
🍌 A banana or any piece of fruit

Rule of thumb: if a snack has more than 5 ingredients or you cannot read the label easily, it is probably not your best choice!

🏆 How It All Connects

💪 Push-ups and rows = stronger groundstrokes and ball striking
🤚 Jump rope = light feet and quick reactions in both sports
🌭 Good nutrition = energy to train hard and recover fast
🧘 Stretching = staying healthy all summer long
