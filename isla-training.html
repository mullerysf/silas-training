⛳ Golf Practice Guide

Short game first — that's where scores are made! Full swing comes after.

🏠 At Home
⛳ At the Range/Course
🏠 Home Practice ~20–25 min
🎯 Putting — 10 min
🏋️‍♀️
Gate Drill
Set 2 tees (or pencils) just wider than your putter face. Roll 20 putts through without hitting them.
💡 Keeps your stroke straight — the #1 skill for making putts
📏
Distance Ladder
Set targets at 3 ft, 6 ft, and 10 ft. Hit 5 putts at each. Count how many you make — try to beat your record!
💡 Track your makes each session and try to beat it next time
🔄
Circle Drill
Place 6 balls in a circle 3 feet from a cup or target. Make all 6 before moving on.
💡 Builds pressure putting — the short ones matter most on the course!
🎪 Swing Mechanics — 10 min (foam balls)
🪷
Slow-Motion Swings
10 swings at 25% speed. Watch your takeaway in a mirror or window. Club stays low and inside — don't pick it up steep.
💡 Slow practice = faster learning
⚖️
Weight Shift Drill
Take your stance. Swing back — feel weight go to back foot. Swing through — weight shifts to front foot. Repeat 15× with a short iron.
💡 Weight shift is the #1 thing that adds distance
🏋️‍♀️
Foam Ball Chip Shots
Set a target (pillow, cup). Chip 20 balls with a 7-iron or wedge. Hands stay ahead of ball, weight on front foot.
💡 This motion is the same as a short pitch shot on the course
💪 Golf Fitness — 5 min
🔄
Trunk Rotations with Club
Hold a club across your shoulders. Rotate slowly left and right × 20. Feel the stretch.
💡 More rotation = more power without swinging harder!
🧘
Hip Hinge Drill
Hold club against your spine. Bend forward from hips (not waist) 15×. This is your golf posture — practice it!
💡 Good posture = consistent ball striking every time
⛳ Range/Course Session ~45–60 min
🎯 Putting Green — 15 min
📏
Lag Putting
Find holes at 20 ft, 30 ft, 40 ft. Hit 5 putts at each. Goal: stop within 2 feet every time. No 3-putts!
💡 Lag putting saves more strokes than any other skill
🎯
5-Hole Make Challenge
Find a hole ~5 feet away. You can't move to the next hole until you make 3 in a row.
💡 Pressure practice = staying calm on the course
🏋️ Chipping Green — 15 min
🎯
Up-and-Down Game
Drop a ball ~10 yards from the flag. Chip, then putt. Count how many you get in 2 shots or less out of 10.
💡 Getting "up and down" is how you save strokes around the green!
🤲
Bump-and-Run vs Lob
Same spot: try a low rolling chip with a 7-iron, then a high lofted wedge chip. 5 of each. See which you're better at!
💡 Knowing which shot fits the situation = smarter golf
😟
Trouble Shots
Drop balls in the rough or on a slope. Practice getting out cleanly — no penalty!
🏋️‍♀️ Driving Range — 20–25 min
🎯
Warm-Up Wedges
Start with 10 easy half-swings with a pitching wedge. No rushing — just feel the contact.
💡 Always start small. Don't try to crush it on swing one!
📊
Structured Practice
10 balls each: 7-iron → 5-iron → hybrid → driver. Pick a specific target for every single shot.
💡 Always aim at something. Random hitting = slow improvement
🔄
Tempo Drill
Count "1-2-3" during your backswing, "4" at the top, "5-6" on the downswing. Hit 10 balls this way.
💡 Smooth tempo = straighter shots. Most beginners rush the downswing!
🏆 Golf Goals This Summer
⛳ Make putts consistently inside 5 feet
🎯 Get up and down from around the green
💪 Build a repeatable, smooth full swing
📏 Learn your distances with each club
😄 Stay calm, have fun, and enjoy the game!

🏆 How It All Connects
🦷 Squats & lunges = power in your golf swing AND tennis split step
🔄 Core + rotation drills = more distance with less effort in golf
⚡ Lateral shuffles = chasing wide shots + footwork around the green
💪 Push-ups & rows = stronger groundstrokes and better ball striking
🤚 Jump rope = light feet and quick reactions in both sports
🧘 Stretching = staying healthy and playing all summer long
